playlist splitter

splits a spotify playlist into genre-based sub-playlists

One-time setup (~2 min)
  1. Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard and create a free app
  2. Under Redirect URIs, add the exact URL of this page
  3. Also add the URL of callback.html (same folder, same host)
  4. Paste your Client ID below and click Connect
